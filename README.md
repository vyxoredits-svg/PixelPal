# PixelPal - Cozy Retro Virtual Pet Companion

PixelPal is a beautifully detailed pixel-art virtual cat companion that lives directly in your browser. Take care of Pixel by feeding, petting, playing, and managing stats (Satiety, Happiness, and Energy) to level up your companion and unlock custom visual items, room upgrades, and achievements!

## 🚀 Key Features

* **32x32 Pixel Art Engine**: Dynamically draws high-fidelity custom pixel art of your cat, including smooth breathing, blinking, tail wagging, and state-specific animations.
* **Coin Economy & Pet Shop**: Earn coins through gameplay actions to purchase foods, toys, accessories, and furniture decorations.
* **Onboarding Tutorial**: First-time players receive an interactive spotlight-driven guide explaining core stats and panels.
* **Three Mini-Games**: Play Catch the Fish, Chase the Yarn, or Memory Match to earn extra coins and XP!
* **Daily & Weekly Quests**: Complete challenges to earn currency, XP, and rare cosmetics.
* **Friendship & Rebirth Systems**: Build a lasting bond with Pixel. Prestige Rebirth resets levels at level 30+ to gain permanent coin and XP multipliers.
* **Live Environmental Cycles**: Experience Day & Night cycles based on real hours and window weather conditions (Sunny, Rainy, Snowy).
* **Save Hardening**: Export and import save progress as JSON files, protected by validation and repair structures.
* **Dev Dashboard Panel**: A hidden analytics control board triggered by clicking the version badge 5 times.
* **Offline Progression**: Fully simulates hours of offline cat sleeping, energy recovery, and stats decay while the browser is closed.
* **Web Audio 8-bit Synth**: Authentic retro sound effects with a toggle switch and custom volume slider.

## 🎮 How to Run

1. Open [index.html](index.html) in any modern web browser.
2. Click actions (Feed, Play, Pet, Sleep) or play games to take care of Pixel.
3. Check the Shop and Closet tabs to customize Pixel's appearance and furniture!
