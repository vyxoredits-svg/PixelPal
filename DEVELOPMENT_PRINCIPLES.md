# PixelPal Development Principles

To maintain the authenticity, charm, and craftsmanship of PixelPal, all future changes must adhere to the following principles. PixelPal must feel like a real indie project built and maintained by a passionate developer.

## Core Principles

### 1. Preserve Quirky Personality
- Avoid sterile interfaces, generic button labels, and corporate placeholder text.
- Maintain the slightly nostalgic, cozy, and organic feel of a virtual pet companion.

### 2. Add Handcrafted Touches
- Focus on micro-animations, playful visual elements, and unexpected interactive details.
- Include personality-driven messages and small hidden surprises (like the developer dashboard triggered by version badge clicks).

### 3. Establish a Clear Opinion
- Stick to PixelPal's cozy purple aesthetic and retro-modern styling.
- Make deliberate design choices that support a consistent visual identity rather than copying generic SaaS dashboard layouts.

### 4. Immersion Over Complexity
- Refine existing feedback, animations, stats simulation, and mechanics.
- Do not solve player experience issues by simply piling on unnecessary new currencies, progression systems, or tabs.

### 5. Document Genuinely
- Write readme, changelogs, and support files in a natural, human developer voice.
- Avoid buzzwords, exaggerated marketing claims, and sterile corporate wording.
